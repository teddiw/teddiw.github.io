About

Hi! I'm a computer science PhD student at Stanford, advised by Carlos Guestrin and supported by the NSF GRFP Fellowship. I often go by Teddi.

I care about making ML models reliable and trustworthy. I'm particularly interested in equipping users to interrogate model generations as model capabilities scale. Recently, I've been thinking about LLM citations, complementary human-AI performance, and what verifiability means to legal professionals!

Previously, I was at UC Berkeley, where I was fortunate to work with Ben Recht and Esther Rolf.
